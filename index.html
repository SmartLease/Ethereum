Why SmartLease ?

SmartLease, is a “smart contract” built on the Ethereum blockchain that creates agreements between landlords and tenants. The benefits are:

  • Encrypted, digital lease agreements between using open-source blockchain code
  • Allows independent verification of a lease contract
  • Instant money transfer upon lease acceptance
